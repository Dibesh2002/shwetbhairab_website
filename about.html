About us

Shree Shwet Bhairab Offset Press is a firm based in Madhyapur Thimi-3, Bhaktapur that offers a full spectrum of graphic design and printing services. Our portfolio includes print design, publishing, advertising, and production for clients of all sizes, from individuals to small businesses, corporations, and non-profits. Our diverse clientele includes specialty restaurants, insurance companies, colleges and universities, financial services firms, publishers, and more. We work within your budget and timeframe to develop powerful, memorable, and effective integrated print and graphic solutions that captivate your audience. In an age of cookie-cutter, prefabricated graphic and print media, we serve clients who seek to differentiate themselves through refined, custom design. Every decision we make involves thoughtful consideration of how to achieve your intended results and meet your needs.

We offer comprehensive services in print and graphic design, taking a personalized approach to create fully custom and unique designs. With extensive experience across multiple industries, we have a proven track record of delivering results for diverse large- and small-scale clients. Our expert print design is backed by years of experience.
